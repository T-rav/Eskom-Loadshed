About SA Loadshed

Monitors Eskom's load shedding status and notifies you when it changes.

This app was listed under the name Eskom Loadshed until Google removed it.

It has been re-released under SA Loadshed to avoid keeping anyone in the dark about the load shedding status.


Developed by StoneAge Technologies.
And still not endorsed by Eskom.
